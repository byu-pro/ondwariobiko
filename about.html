About John Obiko.

I’m a brand and web designer focused on clarity, structure, and long-term brand thinking.

01 / My Approach

I approach design as a strategic discipline, not a decorative one. My work focuses on helping businesses communicate clearly, build credibility, and make better decisions through thoughtful brand and web systems.

I’m particularly interested in the relationship between clarity and trust — how visual structure, typography, and consistency influence perception over time.

02 / Experience

Legacy & Scope

I have over 9 years of experience working across logo design, brand identity, and web design, collaborating with clients at different stages of growth.

My work spans branding systems, websites, and digital touchpoints designed to remain clear, consistent, and adaptable as organizations evolve.

03 / Values

Principled Design

Clarity over complexity
Thoughtful decision-making
Long-term consistency
Respect for context and constraints
04 / Location

Location & Availability

I’m based in Nairobi, Kenya, and work remotely with clients worldwide. I collaborate with teams across different time zones and prioritize clear communication and structured workflows.

05 / Execution

Technical Considerations

In addition to design, I have experience implementing front-end interfaces, which allows me to design systems that translate cleanly into code and remain consistent through execution.

If you’re interested in how this approach translates into real projects, you can explore selected case studies or get in touch to discuss potential work.
