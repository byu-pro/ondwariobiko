Hello, my name is John Obiko, and I am a freelance graphic and web designer based in Nairobi, Kenya. With over 6 years of experience in the design industry, I have had the privilege of working with clients from all over the world, from small startups to large corporations.

My design journey began when I was just a young boy, fascinated by the colors and shapes of the world around me. I would spend hours sketching and painting, trying to capture the beauty of the natural world on paper. As I grew older, I discovered the power of design to communicate ideas, tell stories, and solve problems. I knew that this was the path for me. Over the years, I have developed a deep passion for creating designs that are both beautiful and functional. I believe that great design should not only look good, but also serve a purpose. Whether I am designing a logo, a website, or a marketing campaign, my goal is always to create something that will help my clients achieve their goals and connect with their audience.

In my work, I strive to combine my technical skills with my creative instincts. I am constantly learning and exploring new techniques and technologies, seeking to push the boundaries of what is possible in design. But at the same time, I never lose sight of the human element of design. I believe that design is fundamentally about people, and that the most effective designs are those that connect with people on an emotional level. If you are looking for a designer who is passionate, experienced, and committed to helping you achieve your goals, I would love to hear from you. Please feel free to get in touch to discuss your project and learn more about my design process.
