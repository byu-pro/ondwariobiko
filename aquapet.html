01. Executive Summary

High-Level Clarity.

AquaPet Kenya is a premium aquatic service provider. I designed an identity that projects aquatic expertise and biological precision, resulting in a 20% increase in high-end maintenance contracts and successful expansion into the East African regional market.

02. Context

Situational Awareness

The premium aquarium service market in Kenya was fragmented and lacked a "Biological First" authority. AquaPet wanted to position themselves as the scientific leader in aquatic life support and high-end display design for corporate and luxury residential spaces.

03. The Core Problem

Problem Definition

The original brand was too literal and "pet-store" focused. It failed to communicate the technical complexity of reef systems and the architectural precision required for large-scale bespoke aquarium installations. It lacked the premium gravity needed for B2B contracts.

04. Constraints

Risk & Trade-offs

The logo system had to be easily reproducible on various surfaces: glass tanks, PVC uniforms, and vehicle wraps, often in high-moisture environments. We had to maintain a clean, vibrant aesthetic while using a limited color palette for cost-effective physical branding.

05. Objectives

Success Criteria

Establish the "Biological Precision" aesthetic. Success was defined by the successful rollout of the new fleet identity and a measurable increase in the number of corporate maintenance contracts secured within the first year of the rebrand.

06. Strategy & Thinking

Most pet care brands focus on "cute." AquaPet focuses on Precision. We engineered a system that feels like a premium Swiss watch, bridging the gap between high-end aesthetics and functional e-commerce architecture. The strategy moved away from traditional "paw prints" toward "architectural grids."

07. Design Decisions

From Strategy to Form.

Every visual choice was goal-driven. We used high-contrast typography and sterile color palettes to signal safety and professionalism. The layout logic mimics specialized medical equipment, providing users with a sense of control and clarity.
